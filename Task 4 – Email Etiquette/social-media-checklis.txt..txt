~  Do’s
* Maintain a professional and respectful tone in your posts
* Think before you post—ensure content is appropriate
* Protect your personal information and privacy
* Verify information before sharing it
* Use social media to build a positive digital presence

~ Don’ts
* Do not share offensive, harmful, or inappropriate content
* Avoid oversharing personal or sensitive information
* Do not engage in online arguments or cyberbullying
* Avoid posting misleading or false information
* Do not neglect your academic responsibilities due to social media
